Bribery and Anti-Corruption Policy

1. Purpose

This policy sets out the principles and standards to prevent bribery and corruption within our business operations. It ensures compliance with all applicable anti-bribery laws and reflects our commitment to conducting business ethically and transparently.

⸻

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, officers, directors, contractors, consultants, and third parties acting on behalf of Smarsh, regardless of location.

⸻

3. Policy Statement
	•	Zero Tolerance:
Smarsh has a strict zero-tolerance stance toward bribery and corruption in any form.
	•	Prohibition on Bribes:
No employee or representative may offer, give, solicit, or accept any bribe, kickback, facilitation payment, or improper advantage, whether directly or through a third party.
	•	Gifts & Hospitality:
Gifts, hospitality, or entertainment must never be offered or accepted if they could improperly influence a business decision or create the appearance of impropriety. Modest, infrequent, and transparent exchanges are permitted only if compliant with local laws and this policy.
	•	Third Parties:
Due diligence must be conducted before engaging agents, intermediaries, suppliers, or partners to ensure they share our anti-bribery standards.

⸻

4. Record Keeping

All financial records and supporting documents must be accurate and complete, and maintained in reasonable detail to reflect transactions truthfully. False or misleading entries are strictly prohibited.

⸻

5. Reporting Concerns

Employees are required to promptly report any suspected bribery or unethical conduct. Reports may be made to:
	•	ethics@smarsh.hr
	•	Direct Manager or Compliance Officer

Retaliation against anyone who raises a concern in good faith is strictly prohibited.

⸻

6. Consequences of Non-Compliance

Violation of this policy may lead to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract. Additionally, individuals and the company could face criminal or civil penalties.

⸻

7. Review

This policy will be reviewed periodically to ensure its effectiveness and compliance with applicable laws and best practices.

⸻

Acknowledgment

All employees and representatives are required to read, understand, and adhere to this policy.
